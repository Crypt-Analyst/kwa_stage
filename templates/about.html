What is KwaStage?

KwaStage is Kenya's premier digital platform designed specifically for the boda boda community. We provide comprehensive welfare, emergency support, and financial services that strengthen the bonds within the boda boda family.

Our platform integrates modern technology with traditional community values, ensuring that every rider has access to:

  • Emergency financial support
  • Comprehensive welfare services
  • Community-based loans and savings
  • Digital bike registration and tracking
  • Real-time communication and alerts

How It All Started

The Vision (2024)

Bilford Bwire, a passionate software developer and motorcycle enthusiast, witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by boda boda riders in Kenya. From emergency situations where riders had no financial support to accidents where families were left stranded, he saw the need for a digital solution.

Community Research (Early 2024)

Extensive research was conducted across various boda boda stages in Nairobi, Mombasa, and Kisumu. The feedback was clear: riders needed a trusted, secure platform that could handle welfare contributions, emergency funds, and community communications.

Development Phase (Mid 2024)

Using cutting-edge technology including Django, PostgreSQL, and modern security protocols, KwaStage was built with community input at every stage. The platform was designed to be mobile-first, secure, and easy to use for riders of all technical backgrounds.

Launch & Impact (2025)

KwaStage officially launched with over 1,250 registered members across 45 stages. The platform has since facilitated emergency support for 89 families and managed over KSh 2.5 million in welfare funds, proving its value to the community.
